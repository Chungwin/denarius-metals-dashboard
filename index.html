Risk Assessment

HIGH
Pre-revenue / Cash Burn — Company is pre-commercial production. Significant ongoing capex for Zancudo plant construction while revenue remains limited to bulk concentrate shipments.
HIGH
Dilution Risk — Fully diluted share count (344.7M) is 82% higher than basic (189.4M). Convertible debentures alone could add 68M shares.
HIGH
Execution Risk — Zancudo ramp-up to 1,000 tpd and Aguablanca restart on schedule are critical. Delays could significantly impact valuation.
MEDIUM
Commodity Price Volatility — Revenue is highly sensitive to gold, nickel, copper, and zinc prices. Multi-metal exposure provides some diversification.
MEDIUM
Jurisdiction Risk — Operations in Colombia (security, permitting) and Spain (regulatory). Different risk profiles for each geography.
LOW
EU Strategic Project — Aguablanca's designation as EU Strategic Project provides regulatory support and potential access to EU critical minerals funding.
POSITIVE
Multiple Project Optionality — Four distinct projects across two jurisdictions provide diversification. Warrant exercises (CA$16.7M in early 2026) are strengthening the balance sheet.
